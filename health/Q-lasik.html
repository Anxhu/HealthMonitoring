Q-LASIK

Don’t have time to sit around & wait to recover? With Q-LASIK, you can expect to get back to normal the very next day of laser process. Besides quickly returning to you normal day-to-day activity (this time with clear vision), you won’t need to worry about side effects.

Do More Without Specs

With Q-LASIK surgery, you get freedom from contacts and glasses. Instead of bothering about taking contacts out before bed or seeking endlessly for your lost eyeglasses, you can enjoy your day-to-day life without difficulty. One has to go through a pre Q-Lasik investigation, which ensures eligibility of getting a safe and permanent treatment for removal of specs.

Results Almost Immediately!!!

Most of the patients report 20/20 vision the day after their process. One can resume His/Her day to day activity from the very next day of Refractive Correction/Q-Lasik. Some Individuals may feel/get advice from others that they should rest or stay away from natural/other lights which actually is not needed.

No Bandages or Stitches Required

With Q-LASIK you don’t need to worry about stitches in your eye or to change a bandage. Pair of a disposable goggles is required only for one day and that too just to protect/cover your eyes. It’s vital that you don’t rub or scratch your eyes during the healing process.
