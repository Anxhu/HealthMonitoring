CATARACTS

Cataracts Opacify the eye’s natural lens and limit your ability to see clearly. The lens, a transparent structure in your eye, works like the lens in a camera. Just as a foggy camera lens makes everything dim, a cataract makes it hard for you to see clearly.

What happens???

Cataracts are the most frequent cause of blindness in the world. With availability of new advanced phaco systems, cataract treatment is a very comfortable and painless daycare process. It doesn’t require use of injection, suturing and bandage, enabling individual to start his/her routine activity from the very next day.

Symptoms

  • Cloudy Vision
  • Sensitivity to Light
  • Diffuse Light
  • Colors Appear Faded
  • Double Vision in One Eye
  • Changing Prescription of Glasses

    • Robotic Femto
    • MICS / PHACO
